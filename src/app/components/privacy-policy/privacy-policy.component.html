We follow the Google AdSense Publisher Policies. Third-party vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on a user's prior visits to your website or other websites. Our games (Shape Tactics and Tower Defense) may collect basic technical data like your IP address and browser type for security and analytical purposes. We do not collect personal identification like your real name or address unless you explicitly provide it.

By playing our games, you hereby consent to our Privacy Policy and agree to its terms.

The information collected by log files includes IP addresses, browser type, Internet Service Provider (ISP), date and time stamps, referring/exit pages, and possibly clicks. These are not linked to any information that is personally identifiable.

Our Privacy Policy does not apply to other advertisers or websites. We recommend you consult the respective Privacy Policies of third-party ad servers for more detailed information. For example, Google AdSense uses its own policies and mechanisms for personalized advertising.
